Obtaining BoB troubleshooting info

Please follow the procedure below which will log you in to your old (Athens pre-migrated) BoB account.

  • Open your browser and clear the browser cache and cookies
  • Log in to Bob using this link
  • GO to ‘MYBOB’ and then ‘Preferences’ and see if your email address is still listed in the ‘Account information’ section (and your content is there)
  • Then click this link: http://bobnational.net/athens.php?session_debug=1
  • Copy the output from the page and paste it back to us using the web feedback form
