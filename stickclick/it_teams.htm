The Library IT-Support Team.

What does the IT Support team do?

We are a central team that helps to support IT systems in every UWE Library.

We look after:

  • Unicorn.
  • Ultranet.
  • Simeon.
  • PCs.
  • Printers.
  • User notices (i.e. overdues).
  • Statistics (usage of IT resources).
  • Central equipment inventory.

If things go wrong we may be able to give advice or support but we do not claim to be able to fix everything. When a problem is beyond our capabilities we act as the link between library staff and ITS (UWE's Information Technology Services).

The IT Support Team also provides training on several aspects of IT, including Simeon.

Finally, we are a central source for:

  • PC cleaning materials.
  • Printer cartridges.
  • Floppy disks.
  • Unicorn help sheets.
  • Bar-codes.
  • Other Computer peripherals e.g. scanners, switch boxes etc.

How to Contact the IT Support Team

We live in 3D42 in the Bolland Library at Frenchay, which is just behind the enquiry desk on Level Three. Our telephone extension is 2702. We can also be e-mailed at lib-sys@uwe.ac.uk.
